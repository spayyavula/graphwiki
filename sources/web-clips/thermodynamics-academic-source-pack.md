# Thermodynamics Academic Source Pack

This source pack collects verified academic and university-hosted material for building out the Thermodynamics topic in GraphWiki. The emphasis is on foundational thermodynamics, advanced thermodynamics, and thermodynamics as used in materials, combustion, and computation.

## MIT

- **Thermodynamics & Kinetics**
  - Institution: MIT OpenCourseWare
  - URL: https://ocw.mit.edu/courses/5-60-thermodynamics-kinetics-spring-2008/
  - Type: Course page
  - Why it is useful: A strong foundational course page covering equilibrium thermodynamics, chemical equilibrium, and reaction kinetics.

- **Advanced Thermodynamics**
  - Institution: MIT OpenCourseWare
  - URL: https://ocw.mit.edu/courses/2-43-advanced-thermodynamics-spring-2024/
  - Type: Course page
  - Why it is useful: A modern advanced thermodynamics course that helps extend GraphWiki beyond introductory material.

- **Lecture 1 - Introduction to Thermodynamics**
  - Institution: MIT OpenCourseWare
  - URL: https://ocw.mit.edu/courses/3-020-thermodynamics-of-materials-spring-2021/resources/mit3_020s21_l01/
  - Type: Lecture notes
  - Why it is useful: A concise entry point into entropy, spontaneous processes, and the scope of thermodynamics.

- **Lecture 1: Introduction to Thermodynamics**
  - Institution: MIT OpenCourseWare
  - URL: https://ocw.mit.edu/courses/3-020-thermodynamics-of-materials-spring-2021/resources/mit3_020s21_lecture_01_1080p_v3_mp4/
  - Type: Lecture recording page
  - Why it is useful: Complements the lecture notes with a clearer teaching narrative around entropy, enthalpy, and spontaneous processes.

## Berkeley

  - Type: Course syllabus
  - Why it is useful: A verified course description that is useful for structuring beginner-facing thermodynamics learning paths.

- **ME 254 – Advanced Thermophysics for Applications**
  - Institution: UC Berkeley Mechanical Engineering
  - URL: https://me.berkeley.edu/wp-content/uploads/2019/05/ME-254-10.22.18.pdf
  - Type: Course syllabus
  - Why it is useful: Extends the topic from fundamentals into energy conversion systems and applied thermophysical analysis.

- **Entropy and Partial Differential Equations**
  - Institution: UC Berkeley Mathematics
  - URL: https://math.berkeley.edu/~evans/entropy.and.PDE.pdf

## Princeton

  - URL: https://cefrc.princeton.edu/sites/g/files/toruqf1071/files/2018_pitsch_lecture2.pdf
  - Type: Lecture notes
  - Why it is useful: A strong applied thermodynamics source grounded in combustion and equilibrium analysis.

- **Thermodynamics, Flame Temperature and Equilibrium (2014 notes)**

## Notes

- The strongest foundational sources in this pack are the MIT thermodynamics courses, Berkeley ME 40, and Princeton combustion thermodynamics notes.
- The strongest advanced sources are the Princeton dynamical systems chapter and the Caltech thermodynamic computing paper.
